Dendritic Learning

Dendritic Learning is a studying app built on the ideas of making learning more natural, efficient, and accessible. It uses a variety of new methods that allow it to better teach most subjects, such as writing recognition, flowchart-style 'web sets', drawing, flashcards, and more. It is currently being tested in a closed beta at some high schools, and will soon be released for all students.

Park214

Starting as my first exploration in machine learning, Park214 is an iOS app built for security guards to quickly check the status of cars parked in a specific lot. By using machine learning, it recognizes license plates and confirms whether or not a specific vehicle is in the given dataset. It is currently being implemented in District 214 schools as an update their system of parking passes.

NASA ADC 2025

The NASA App Development Challenge is a yearly national coding competition where students build applications to solve problems given by NASA. The 2025 challenge centered around processing and displaying the data of the Artemis II mission. Our app, created using the Unity game engine, is a flexible, easy-to-use, and powerful way to visualize the mission data. The contest is currently being judged, but results will come out soon!
