NASA ADC 2025

National Winner

The NASA App Development Challenge is a yearly national coding competition where students build applications to solve problems given by NASA. The 2025 challenge centered around processing and displaying the data of the Artemis II mission. Our app, created using the Unity game engine, is a flexible, easy-to-use, and powerful way to visualize the mission data. We were selected as one of four top teams nationally, and presented it in Houston at NASA.
